**SOCIETIES AND CULTURES OF THE ENGLISH- SPEAKING WORLD**

Europe

# Europe: General Information

Three areas considered part of the Outer Circle:

- Gibraltar
- Malta
- Cyprus

Gibraltar

- British overseas territory
- Located on a narrow peninsula on the southern coast of Spain
- Northeast of the Straits of Gibraltar
- Conquered by the British in 1704

# Gibraltar

- An issue of dispute between Britain and Spain ever since
- The 2002 referendum: the vast majority of the population voted for Gibraltar to remain a British dependency
- English is the official language
- A local Spanish dialect is also spoken

# Malta

- An island nation in the Mediterranean Sea
- British colony from 1814 to 1964
- Today an independent EU member state

# Malta

- British influence is still felt
- English is one of the two official languages
- The other official language: Maltese
- Maltese is the only Semitic language written in the Latin alphabet

# Cyprus

- Island nation in the Mediterranean Sea
- EU member state
- Under British control from 1878 to 1960
- Independence: Escalation of the conflict between the two main ethnic groups on

the island (the Greeks and the Turks)

- Northern part of the island was invaded by Turkish troops in 1974

# Cyprus

- Independence of the northern part of the island declared in 1983
- Independent Turkish Cypriot state recognized only by Turkey
- Remains divided
- Official languages: both Greek and Turkish
- English is widely spoken
