# Contradictions and ambiguities

Track places where sources differ in framing, emphasis, or factual detail.

## Current known tensions

- Some modern commentary pieces conflict in tone with textbook-style summaries.
- Outline files can oversimplify chronology compared with long-form sources.

## Handling rule

If conflicting claims appear:

1. report both claims,
2. prefer the more specific and better-contextualized passage,
3. cite both paths when uncertainty remains.
