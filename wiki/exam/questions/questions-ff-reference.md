# Questions FF Reference

This page normalizes the question style from:

- `questions_ff/Izpit Družbe in kulture - enopredmetni - ustni del.md`
- `questions_ff/Izpit Dr in kulture - dvopredmetni - ustni del.md`

## Canonical prompt types

1. Comment on the concept of the **British Empire** (major colonies; meaning of **Commonwealth**).
2. Comment on key events in British history: Anglo-Saxon, Norman, English Renaissance, Civil War, Protectorate, Restoration, Victorian era.
3. Present at least three representatives (and works) from selected literary periods.
4. Name at least one Anglo-Saxon and one Norman monarch; explain their significance.
5. Explain what **Magna Carta** and the **Domesday Book** mark in British history.
6. Fast factual forms: **When was X? Who wrote X? What happened in X?**
