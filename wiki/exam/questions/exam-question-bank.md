# Exam Question Bank

Normalized from `questions_ff` prompts. Detailed mapping: [Questions FF Reference](questions-ff-reference.md).

## Core prompts (English)

1. Comment on the term *British Empire* (major colonies and the idea of the Commonwealth).
2. Explain major periods in British history: Anglo-Saxon, Norman, Renaissance, Civil War, Protectorate, Restoration, Victorian.
3. Present at least three writers (and works) from one literary period.
4. Name one Anglo-Saxon and one Norman monarch and discuss their impact.
5. Explain the significance of Magna Carta and the Domesday Book.
6. When was X and why does it matter?
7. Who wrote X and what is the work about?
8. What happened in X and what were the consequences?
