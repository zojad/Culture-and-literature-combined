# English as L1, L2, and Foreign Language

## Scope note

Use this page for the FF prompt about English as a first, second, or foreign language in former British colonies. The strongest local anchor is Kachru's model.

## Exam answer frame

- Explain Inner, Outer, and Expanding Circle
- Distinguish L1, L2/ESL, and EFL roles
- Relate the spread of English to British colonial history
- Add one concrete regional example from the corpus
- Mention that official status and everyday first language may differ
